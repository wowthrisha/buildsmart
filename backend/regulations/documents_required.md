## Documents Required for Building Plan Approval — CCMC Coimbatore

### Mandatory Documents for Residential Building Permit

The following documents must be submitted to the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation for building plan approval:

**Title Documents:**
Sale Deed or registered title document proving ownership of the plot. The sale deed must be registered at the Sub-Registrar office and must clearly identify the plot boundaries.

Patta is the revenue document issued by the Revenue Department confirming the owner's name in revenue records. Chitta shows the survey number and area. Both patta and chitta must be in the applicant's name or a name-transfer must be initiated before applying.

Encumbrance Certificate (EC) must be obtained from the Sub-Registrar office and must be dated within 30 days of the building plan application date. The EC confirms there are no mortgages, loans, or legal encumbrances on the property.

**Survey Documents:**
FMB Sketch (Field Measurement Book Sketch) is the official survey map issued by the Survey Department showing the plot boundaries and measurements. It must match the dimensions in the site plan.

**Design Documents:**
Site plan showing the plot, proposed building footprint, setbacks, road abutment, and north direction. Must be prepared in eDCR (electronic Development Control Rules) format for online submission.

Building plan drawings including ground floor plan, upper floor plans, elevation drawings, and section drawings. Prepared and signed by a licensed architect or engineer registered with CCMC.

Architect Certificate signed by a licensed architect stating that the proposed building complies with TNCDBR 2019 rules and the approved plan.

**Tax and Identity Documents:**
Property Tax Receipt for the current year showing the plot is assessed for property tax.

Aadhaar card of the property owner as identity proof.

### Online Submission via eDCR

CCMC accepts building plan applications through the eDCR (electronic Development Control Rules) portal. Plans must be drawn in AutoCAD format and submitted digitally. The system automatically checks for setback and FAR compliance.

### Approval Timeline

Standard residential building plans in CCMC jurisdiction are typically approved within 3 to 7 working days for plots within city limits that comply with all TNCDBR 2019 norms.

Incomplete applications or applications with compliance violations will be rejected and must be resubmitted after corrections.
