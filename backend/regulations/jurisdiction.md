## Jurisdiction and Approving Authorities — Tamil Nadu

### CCMC — Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation

CCMC has jurisdiction over all plots within the Coimbatore city limits. CCMC approves building plans for residential and commercial buildings within its boundaries.

Plots within Coimbatore city limits with area below 2.47 acres (1 hectare) and building height below 14 metres (non-high-rise) require CCMC approval only.

CCMC helpline: 0422-2390261. Typical approval time for standard residential plans: 3 to 7 working days.

CCMC applies TNCDBR 2019 rules for all building plan approvals. The eDCR portal is used for online submission.

### DTCP — Directorate of Town and Country Planning

DTCP approval is required for layouts and subdivisions with total area exceeding 2.47 acres (1 hectare or 10,000 sq.m). DTCP also approves township projects and large commercial developments.

If your plot is part of a layout that was carved out of land exceeding 2.47 acres without DTCP approval, the layout itself may be unauthorised and individual building permits cannot be issued until the layout is regularised.

DTCP helpline: 044-29585247. Typical approval time: 4 to 6 months for layout approval.

### LPA — Local Planning Authority

For plots in peri-urban areas, outskirts, and areas outside CCMC boundaries but within the Coimbatore Local Planning Area, the LPA (Local Planning Authority) has jurisdiction. LPA applications are processed through the DTCP regional office.

### Jurisdiction Determination

To determine which authority has jurisdiction over your plot:
1. Check if the plot is within CCMC ward boundaries
2. If yes and area is below 2.47 acres — apply to CCMC
3. If plot is in a layout exceeding 2.47 acres — DTCP approval required
4. If plot is outside CCMC limits — check with LPA/DTCP regional office

Source: Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, TNCDBR 2019. G.O.Ms.No.18, MA&WS, 04-02-2019.
