# TNCDBR 2019 Amendments Summary

## G.O.Ms.No.16, Municipal Administration Dept, 31 January 2020
Rule 35 setback table fully replaced.
Minimum road width for access reduced from 7.2m to 6.0m.
Commercial zone outside CMA: roads 9m and above deemed commercial.
Parking for single house up to 300 sq.m capped at 2 car + 2 two-wheeler spaces.
Permission for buildings under 1200 sq.ft must be issued within 30 days.
AC ledge up to 0.6m permitted within setback space.

## G.O.Ms.No.69 and G.O.Ms.No.70, Housing and Urban Development Dept, 11 March 2024
Non-high-rise maximum height raised from 12m to 14m.
High-rise building redefined as exceeding 14m (previously 12m).
Completion certificate exemption extended to buildings up to 14m, 8 units, 750 sq.m.
Security deposit exemption extended to same criteria.

## G.O.Ms.No.133, Housing and Urban Development Dept, 18 July 2024
Self-certification scheme for small residential buildings introduced.
Eligible: plots up to 2500 sq.ft, built-up up to 3500 sq.ft, max 2 units, G+1 only.
Simplified online process, no multiple authority approvals required.

## G.O.Ms.No.154, Housing and Urban Development Dept, 07 October 2025
Cottage Industries renamed to White Industries throughout TNCDBR.
Road width for White and Green industries in Village Panchayat areas reduced to 6m.
Orange and Red industries still require 7m minimum road width.
No changes to residential setback, FAR, or height rules in this GO.
