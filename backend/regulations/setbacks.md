# TNCDBR Setback Requirements — Non-High-Rise Residential Buildings
## Source: Rule 35, G.O.Ms.No.16, Municipal Administration Dept, 31 January 2020

### Applicability
Non-high-rise buildings up to 16 dwellings, small commercial up to 300 sq.m.
Coimbatore CCMC falls under "Other areas" column.

### FRONT SETBACK — Other Areas (Coimbatore)
| Abutting Road Width | Front Setback Required |
|---|---|
| Up to 9m (approx 30ft) | 1.5m |
| More than 9m up to 18m | 3.0m |
| More than 18m up to 30.5m | 4.5m |
| More than 30.5m | 6.0m |

### SIDE SETBACK — Other Areas
| Building Height | Plot Width | Side Setback |
|---|---|---|
| Up to 7m | Up to 9m | 1m on one side |
| Up to 7m | Above 9m | 1m either side OR 2m one side |
| More than 7m up to 12m | Up to 6m | 1m on one side |
| More than 7m up to 12m | Above 6m up to 9m | 1.5m on one side |
| More than 7m up to 12m | Above 9m | 1.5m either side OR 3m one side |

### REAR SETBACK
| Building Height | Rear Setback |
|---|---|
| Up to 7m | Nil |
| More than 7m up to 12m | 1.5m |

### Continuous Building Areas
Front: 1.5m | Side: Nil | Rear: Nil

### Economically Weaker Section Areas
Front: 1.0m | Side: Nil | Rear: Nil
