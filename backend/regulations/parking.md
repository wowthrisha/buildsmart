## Parking Requirements — TNCDBR 2019

### Residential Parking

Residential buildings must provide a minimum of 1 car parking space per dwelling unit.

For individual residential plots (single house), one car parking space must be provided within the plot. This space is typically accommodated as an open car park within the front setback area or as a covered stilt parking.

For apartment buildings, 1 car parking space per flat is mandatory. A building with 4 flats must have at least 4 car parking spaces within the plot.

### Commercial Parking

Commercial buildings must provide a minimum of 2 car parking spaces per 100 sq.m of built-up area.

A commercial building with 500 sq.m of built-up area requires at least 10 car parking spaces.

### Parking Space Dimensions

Each car parking space must have minimum dimensions of 2.5m width and 5.0m length. The access driveway to parking areas must have a minimum width of 3.5m.

### Stilt Parking

Stilt parking (open ground floor used exclusively for parking) is a common solution in Coimbatore. The stilt floor is typically excluded from FAR calculation when used exclusively for parking, provided it meets the height and dimension requirements.

Stilt floor height must be a minimum of 2.5m clear height to qualify for FAR exclusion.

### Two-Wheeler Parking

Two-wheeler parking requirements are separate from car parking. Residential buildings with more than 4 units should provide designated two-wheeler parking within the plot.

Source: TNCDBR 2019, Parking Regulations. G.O.Ms.No.18, MA&WS, 04-02-2019.
